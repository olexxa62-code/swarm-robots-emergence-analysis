# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in the A.2 Swarm Robots Kappa Analysis project a respectful and professional experience for everyone, regardless of background or level of expertise.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

* Using professional and academic language
* Providing constructive criticism with scientific basis
* Focusing on objective, reproducible results
* Respecting differing scientific viewpoints
* Acknowledging contributions and citations properly

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

* Unprofessional or inappropriate language
* Publishing others' work without proper attribution
* Falsifying data or results
* Dismissing scientific criticism without basis
* Any form of harassment or discrimination

## Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying standards and taking appropriate corrective action in response to unacceptable behavior.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces, including repository, issues, and any project communication channels.

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to the project team. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.

## Attribution

This Code of Conduct is adapted for academic research projects, emphasizing scientific integrity and professional collaboration.
